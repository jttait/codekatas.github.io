Poker hands from highest to lowest:

  • Royal Flush: 10, J, Q, K, A. All same suit.
  • Straight Flush: Five cards in a sequence. All same suit.
  • Four of a Kind: Four cards, all same rank.
  • Full House: Three of a kind and one pair.
  • Flush: All five cards of same suit.
  • Straight: All five cards in a sequence.
  • Three of a Kind: Three cards of same rank.
  • Two Pair: Two different pairs.
  • One Pair: Two cards of same rank.
  • High Card: When you haven't made any of the hands above.
