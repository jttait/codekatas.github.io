CodeKata > CoinChange

A vending machine dispenses change to customers after a purchase.

Various denominations of coins may be available to make this change.

Various amounts of each denomination may be available.

Provide change using the least number of coins.

For example, change of $1.30 with available coins of 1×50¢, 10×25¢, 5×5¢, and 6×1¢ should return 1×50¢, 3×25¢, and 1×5¢.
