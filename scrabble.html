Kata > Scrabble

A player can place up to eight tiles.

These tiles are worth 1 point: A, E, I, L, N, O, R, S, T, U.

These tiles are worth 2 points: D, G.

These tiles are worth 3 points: B, C, M, P.

These tiles are worth 4 points: F, H, V, W, Y.

These tiles are worth 5 points: K.

These tiles are worth 8 points: J, X.

These tiles are worth 10 points: Q, Z.

A wildcard tile is worth 0 points.

Premium squares are Triple Word Score, Double Word Score, Triple Letter Score, and Double Letter Score.

If the word occupies two premium word squares then the score is multiplied by both e.g. multiply by 9 for two Triple Word Scores.
