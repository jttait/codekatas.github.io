CodeKata > TenPinBowling

If all 10 pins are knocked down on the first shot then the frame ends and you get a strike.

A strike earns 10 points plus the sum of the next two shots.

If all 10 pins are knocked down using both shots then you get a spare.

A spare earns 10 points plus the sum of the next shot.

If all 10 pins are not knocked down then you get an open frame.

An open frame scores the number of pins knocked down.

If you roll a strike on the first shot on the first shot of the 10th frame then you get 2 more shots.

If you roll a spare on the first two shots of the 10th frame then you get 1 more shot.

The score for the 10th frame is the total number of pins knocked down.

Keep track of the score during a bowling game.
